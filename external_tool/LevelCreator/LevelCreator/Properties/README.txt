Usage:
The program allows for the creation of a level through individual tiles in a wall of spaces. 
These spaces are filled out by clicking on them when a template is clicked on

The templates represent different objects and tiles necessary for creating a level in the game.
Clicking on any of the buttons for the templates will allow the user to then paste their clicked 
template onto individual buttons in the wall of buttons.

The white template is used to change the state of the object or tile in the button. This allows
each button to be edited individually.

Upon saving the file, the format will be uploaded as a wall of characters with the dimensions on top.

EX: 20,15

The characters are determined by the color and orientation of the corresponding button in the program.
The character in the button determines its rotation.

------------------------------------------
THESE ORIENTATIONS ARE AS SHOWN:

1 - DEFAULT
2 - 90 degree rotation
3 - 180 degree rotation
4 - 270 degree rotation

H - Horizontal reflection
V - Vertical reflection

G - Goblin enemy
S - Slime enemy
W - Wizard enemy
